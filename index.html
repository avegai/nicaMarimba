Instructions

  • A thru L (C to C) trigger single notes, so you can basically play the marimba as a piano.
  • 1-4 trigger loops samples
  • 5-8 activates effects, reverses and processes the aforementioned samples in different ways, try it out!
  • Mouse clicks trigger a nice crunchy effect on the percussion.
